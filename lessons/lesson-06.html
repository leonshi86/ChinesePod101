💬 Dialogue - At a Restaurant

服务员:
请问几位？
Qǐngwèn jǐ wèi?
How many people?
你:
两位。
Liǎng wèi.
Two people.
服务员:
想吃什么？
Xiǎng chī shénme?
What would you like to eat?
你:
我要一碗面条和一份饺子。
Wǒ yào yì wǎn miàntiáo hé yí fèn jiǎozi.
I want a bowl of noodles and a plate of dumplings.
服务员:
喝什么？
Hē shénme?
What to drink?
你:
请给我一杯茶。
Qǐng gěi wǒ yì bēi chá.
Please give me a cup of tea.
服务员:
好的，马上来。
Hǎo de, mǎshàng lái.
OK, coming right up.

📝 Grammar

我要 + quantity + food

我要一碗面条。= I want a bowl of noodles.
我要两杯咖啡。= I want two cups of coffee.

请给我 + quantity + item

请给我一杯茶。= Please give me a cup of tea.
请给我三个饺子。= Please give me three dumplings.
