Lesson 21: 求职面试 Intermediate

Learn essential Chinese phrases for job interviews, self-introduction, and discussing qualifications.

⏱ 10 minutes 📚 Intermediate 🎯 Career
0:00 / 10:00

💬 Dialogue

面试官:
请先自我介绍一下。
Qǐng xiān zìwǒ jièshào yīxià.
Please introduce yourself first.
应聘者:
您好，我叫李华，毕业于北京大学，专业是计算机科学。我有三年的软件开发经验。
Nín hǎo, wǒ jiào Lǐ Huá, bìyè yú Běijīng Dàxué, zhuānyè shì jìsuànjī kēxué. Wǒ yǒu sān nián de ruǎnjiàn kāifā jīngyàn.
Hello, my name is Li Hua. I graduated from Peking University, majoring in Computer Science. I have three years of software development experience.
面试官:
你为什么想加入我们公司？你对自己的职业规划是什么？
Nǐ wèishénme xiǎng jiārù wǒmen gōngsī? Nǐ duì zìjǐ de zhíyè guīhuà shì shénme?
Why do you want to join our company? What is your career plan?
应聘者:
贵公司在行业内很有影响力，我希望能在这样的环境中学习和成长。我的目标是成为技术专家。
Guì gōngsī zài hángyè nèi hěn yǒu yǐngxiǎnglì, wǒ xīwàng néng zài zhèyàng de huánjìng zhōng xuéxí hé chéngzhǎng. Wǒ de mùbiāo shì chéngwéi jìshù zhuānjiā.
Your company is very influential in the industry. I hope to learn and grow in such an environment. My goal is to become a technical expert.
面试官:
你期望的薪资是多少？什么时候可以入职？
Nǐ qīwàng de xīnzī shì duōshao? Shénme shíhou kěyǐ rùzhí?
What is your expected salary? When can you start?
应聘者:
根据我的经验和市场情况，期望月薪在一万五左右。如果录用，我可以两周内入职。
Gēnjù wǒ de jīngyàn hé shìchǎng qíngkuàng, qīwàng yuèxīn zài yī wàn wǔ zuǒyòu. Rúguǒ lùyòng, wǒ kěyǐ liǎng zhōu nèi rùzhí.
Based on my experience and market conditions, I expect a monthly salary around 15,000 yuan. If hired, I can start within two weeks.

📝 Grammar Points

1. 毕业 (bìyè) + 于 (yú) - Graduated from

Used to state where you graduated from.

Examples:
我毕业于清华大学。 → I graduated from Tsinghua University.
他毕业于哈佛大学。 → He graduated from Harvard University.

2. 贵 (guì) - Honorific prefix

Used to show respect when referring to someone else's company, name, etc.

Examples:
贵公司 → Your company (polite)
贵姓 → Your surname (polite)
贵国 → Your country (polite)

3. 如果...可以... (rúguǒ...kěyǐ...) - If..., can...

Conditional structure for stating possibilities.

Examples:
如果录用，我可以下周入职。 → If hired, I can start next week.
如果通过面试，我可以立即上班。 → If I pass the interview, I can start immediately.
