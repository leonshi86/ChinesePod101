💬 Dialogue - At the Doctor

医生:
你怎么了？
Nǐ zěnme le?
What's wrong with you?
你:
我不舒服，头疼，还发烧。
Wǒ bù shūfu, tóuténg, hái fāshāo.
I don't feel well, headache, and fever.
医生:
咳嗽吗？
Késou ma?
Do you have a cough?
你:
有一点儿。
Yǒu yìdiǎnr.
A little.
医生:
你感冒了。吃这个药，多喝水，多休息。
Nǐ gǎnmào le. Chī zhège yào, duō hēshuǐ, duō xiūxi.
You have a cold. Take this medicine, drink more water, rest more.
你:
好的，谢谢医生。
Hǎo de, xièxie yīsheng.
OK, thank you doctor.

📝 Grammar Points

1. 你怎么了？(What's wrong?)

Ask about someone's condition.
你怎么了？= What's wrong with you?
他怎么了？= What's wrong with him?

2. Body Part + 疼 (Pain)

Describe where it hurts.
头疼 = headache
肚子疼 = stomachache
牙疼 = toothache

3. 多 + Verb (Do more...)

Give advice.
多喝水 = drink more water
多休息 = rest more

💡 Doctor's Advice Phrases

• 多喝水 = drink more water
• 多休息 = get more rest
• 别太累 = don't overwork
• 按时吃药 = take medicine on time
