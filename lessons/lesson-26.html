Lesson 26: 网购与快递 Intermediate

Learn Chinese for online shopping and delivery services.

💬 Dialogue

买家:
这件衣服有别的颜色吗？我想换成红色的。
Zhè jiàn yīfu yǒu bié de yánsè ma? Wǒ xiǎng huàn chéng hóngse de.
Does this clothing come in other colors? I want to change to red.
客服:
有的，您可以申请换货。请问快递单号是多少？
Yǒu de, nín kěyǐ shēnqǐng huànhuò. Qǐngwèn kuàidì dānhào shì duōshao?
Yes, you can apply for an exchange. What's your tracking number?
买家:
我已经收到货了，但是尺寸不太合适。
Wǒ yǐjīng shōudào huò le, dànshì chǐcùn bù tài héshì.
I received it, but the size doesn't fit well.
