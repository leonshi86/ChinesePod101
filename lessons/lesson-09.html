💬 Dialogue - Taking a Taxi

你:
师傅，去火车站。
Shīfu, qù huǒchēzhàn.
Driver, to the train station.
司机:
好的，请系好安全带。
Hǎo de, qǐng xì hǎo ānquándài.
OK, please fasten your seatbelt.
你:
大概要多久？
Dàgài yào duōjiǔ?
About how long will it take?
司机:
二十分钟左右。
Èrshí fēnzhōng zuǒyòu.
About 20 minutes.
你:
好的，谢谢。
Hǎo de, xièxie.
OK, thanks.

💬 Dialogue - Buying a Ticket

你:
我要买一张去北京的票。
Wǒ yào mǎi yì zhāng qù Běijīng de piào.
I want to buy a ticket to Beijing.
售票员:
要几点的？
Yào jǐ diǎn de?
What time?
你:
下午两点左右的。
Xiàwǔ liǎng diǎn zuǒyòu de.
Around 2 PM.
售票员:
好的，三百五十块。
Hǎo de, sānbǎi wǔshí kuài.
OK, 350 Yuan.

📝 Grammar Points

1. 去 + Place (Go to...)

去北京 = go to Beijing
去机场 = go to the airport
去火车站 = go to the train station

2. 坐 + Transportation (Take...)

坐地铁 = take the subway
坐公交车 = take the bus
坐出租车 = take a taxi
坐飞机 = fly / take a plane

3. 要多久？(How long does it take?)

要去多久？= How long will it take?
大概二十分钟。= About 20 minutes.

💡 Chinese Transportation Tips

• "师傅" (shīfu) is a polite way to address taxi drivers
• China's high-speed trains are called "高铁" (gāotiě)
• Most cities use mobile payment for public transport
