Lesson 1: 你好 (Hello)

Learn how to greet people and introduce yourself in Chinese.

⏱ 5 minutes 📚 Beginner 🎯 Survival Chinese
0:00 / 5:00

💬 Dialogue

A:
你好！
Nǐ hǎo!
Hello!
B:
你好！
Nǐ hǎo!
Hello!
A:
我叫李明。你叫什么名字？
Wǒ jiào Lǐ Míng. Nǐ jiào shénme míngzi?
My name is Li Ming. What's your name?
B:
我叫王华。
Wǒ jiào Wáng Huá.
My name is Wang Hua.
A:
很高兴认识你！
Hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ!
Nice to meet you!
B:
我也很高兴认识你！再见！
Wǒ yě hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ! Zàijiàn!
Nice to meet you too! Goodbye!

📝 Grammar Points

1. 你好 (Nǐ hǎo) - Hello

The most common greeting in Chinese. Literally means "you good".

Example:
你好！ → Hello!

2. 我叫... (Wǒ jiào...) - My name is...

Structure: 我 (I) + 叫 (called) + name

Example:
我叫李明。 → My name is Li Ming.

3. Question: 你叫什么名字？ (Nǐ jiào shénme míngzi?)

Structure: 你 (you) + 叫 (called) + 什么 (what) + 名字 (name)

Example:
你叫什么名字？ → What is your name?
