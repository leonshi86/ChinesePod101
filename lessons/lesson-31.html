Lesson 31: 烹饪与食谱 Intermediate

Learn Chinese for cooking and recipes.

💬 Dialogue

A:
这道菜怎么做？
Zhè dào cài zěnme zuò?
How do you make this dish?
B:
先把油加热，然后放入葱姜蒜爆香，再把肉放进去炒。
Xiān bǎ yóu jiārè, ránhòu fàngrù cōngjiāngsuàn bàoxiāng, zài bǎ ròu fàng jìnqù chǎo.
First heat the oil, then add green onion, ginger and garlic to release the aroma, then add the meat to stir-fry.
