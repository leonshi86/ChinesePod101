💬 Dialogue - Making a Call

A:
喂，你好！
Wéi, nǐ hǎo!
Hello!
B:
你好，请问你是哪位？
Nǐ hǎo, qǐngwèn nǐ shì nǎ wèi?
Hello, who is this?
A:
我是小明，王老师在吗？
Wǒ shì Xiǎomíng, Wáng lǎoshī zài ma?
This is Xiaoming, is Teacher Wang there?
B:
请稍等。
Qǐng shāoděng.
Please wait a moment.
A:
好的，谢谢。
Hǎo de, xièxie.
OK, thanks.

💬 Dialogue - Wrong Number

A:
喂，你好！
Wéi, nǐ hǎo!
Hello!
B:
你好，请问是张先生吗？
Nǐ hǎo, qǐngwèn shì Zhāng xiānsheng ma?
Hello, is this Mr. Zhang?
A:
不是，你打错了。
Bú shì, nǐ dǎ cuò le.
No, you have the wrong number.
B:
对不起。
Duìbuqǐ.
Sorry.
A:
没关系。
Méi guānxi.
That's OK.

📝 Grammar Points

1. 喂 (Wéi) - Phone Greeting

Used only when answering or making phone calls.
喂，你好！= Hello! (on phone)

2. 我是... (This is...)

Introduce yourself on the phone.
我是小明。= This is Xiaoming.
我是李华。= This is Li Hua.

3. ...在吗？(Is ... there?)

Ask if someone is available.
王老师在吗？= Is Teacher Wang there?
你爸爸在吗？= Is your dad there?
