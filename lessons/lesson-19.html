Lesson 19: 银行业务 Intermediate

Learn how to handle banking transactions, open accounts, and manage money in Chinese.

⏱ 9 minutes 📚 Intermediate 🎯 Banking & Finance
0:00 / 9:00

💬 Dialogue

顾客:
您好，我想开一个银行账户。
Nín hǎo, wǒ xiǎng kāi yī gè yínháng zhànghù.
Hello, I'd like to open a bank account.
柜员:
好的。请问您需要储蓄账户还是支票账户？
Hǎo de. Qǐngwèn nín xūyào chǔxù zhànghù háishì zhīpiào zhànghù?
Sure. Do you need a savings account or a checking account?
顾客:
储蓄账户。请问有什么要求吗？
Chǔxù zhànghù. Qǐngwèn yǒu shénme yāoqiú ma?
A savings account. What are the requirements?
柜员:
请出示您的身份证或护照，还有手机号码。最低存款是10元。
Qǐng chūshì nín de shēnfènzhèng huò hùzhào, háiyǒu shǒujī hàomǎ. Zuìdī cúnkuǎn shì 10 yuán.
Please show your ID card or passport, and your phone number. Minimum deposit is 10 yuan.
顾客:
好的，这是我的护照。我还可以办理网上银行吗？
Hǎo de, zhè shì wǒ de hùzhào. Wǒ hái kěyǐ bànlǐ wǎngshàng yínháng ma?
Okay, here's my passport. Can I also set up online banking?
柜员:
当然可以。这是您的银行卡和网银密码器。请在这里签字确认。
Dāngrán kěyǐ. Zhè shì nín de yínhángkǎ hé wǎngyín mìmǎqì. Qǐng zài zhèlǐ qiānzì quèrèn.
Of course. Here's your bank card and online banking token. Please sign here to confirm.

📝 Grammar Points

1. 开账户 (kāi zhànghù) - To open an account

Common banking phrase. 开 (kāi) means "to open" in the sense of starting something.

Examples:
我想开一个银行账户。 → I want to open a bank account.
开信用卡 → to open a credit card

2. 还是 (háishì) - Or

Used in questions to present alternatives.

Examples:
储蓄账户还是支票账户？ → Savings account or checking account?
您要喝茶还是咖啡？ → Would you like tea or coffee?

3. 可以...吗？ (kěyǐ...ma?) - Can I...?

Used to ask for permission or ability.

Examples:
我可以办理网上银行吗？ → Can I set up online banking?
可以用信用卡吗？ → Can I use a credit card?
