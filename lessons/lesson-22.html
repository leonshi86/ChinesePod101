💬 Dialogue

同事A:
早上好！今天的会议几点开始？
Zǎoshang hǎo! Jīntiān de huìyì jǐ diǎn kāishǐ?
Good morning! What time does today's meeting start?
同事B:
早上好！上午十点，在会议室A。记得带你的报告。
Zǎoshang hǎo! Shàngwǔ shí diǎn, zài huìyìshì A. Jìde dài nǐ de bàogào.
Good morning! 10 AM, in Meeting Room A. Remember to bring your report.
同事A:
你能帮我打印一份吗？打印机好像坏了。
Nǐ néng bāng wǒ dǎyìn yī fèn ma? Dǎyìnjī hǎoxiàng huài le.
Can you help me print a copy? The printer seems broken.
同事B:
好的。对了，老板让你下午去他办公室一趟。
Hǎo de. Duìle, lǎobǎn ràng nǐ xiàwǔ qù tā bàngōngshì yī tàng.
Okay. By the way, the boss wants you to go to his office this afternoon.
同事A:
谢谢提醒。下班后要不要一起吃饭？
Xièxie tíxǐng. Xiàbān hòu yào bu yào yīqǐ chīfàn?
Thanks for the reminder. Want to have dinner together after work?

📝 Grammar Points

1. 记得 (jìde) - Remember to

Used to remind someone to do something.

记得带报告。 → Remember to bring the report.
记得开会。 → Remember to attend the meeting.

2. 好像 (hǎoxiàng) - Seems like; appears to

Used to express uncertainty or observation.

打印机好像坏了。 → The printer seems broken.
他好像不太舒服。 → He seems not feeling well.

3. 去...一趟 (qù...yī tàng) - Go there once

Indicates a short trip or visit.

去办公室一趟。 → Go to the office once.
去超市一趟。 → Go to the supermarket once.
