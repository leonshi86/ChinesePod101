📝 Grammar Points

1. 几岁 (jǐ suì) - Asking age

Used for children or young people. For adults, use 您多大？ (Nín duō dà?)

2. 电话号码 (diànhuà hàomǎ)

Phone numbers are read digit by digit: 一三八... not 一百三十八
