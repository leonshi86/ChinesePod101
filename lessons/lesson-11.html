💬 Dialogue - Talking About Your Day

A:
你每天几点起床？
Nǐ měitiān jǐ diǎn qǐchuáng?
What time do you get up every day?
B:
我七点起床。
Wǒ qī diǎn qǐchuáng.
I get up at 7 o'clock.
A:
几点上班？
Jǐ diǎn shàngbān?
What time do you go to work?
B:
九点上班，六点下班。
Jiǔ diǎn shàngbān, liù diǎn xiàbān.
I start work at 9, get off at 6.
A:
晚上做什么？
Wǎnshang zuò shénme?
What do you do in the evening?
B:
吃饭、看电视、睡觉。
Chīfàn, kàn diànshì, shuìjiào.
Eat, watch TV, sleep.

📝 Grammar Points

1. 几点 + Verb? (What time do you...?)

Ask about time of activities.
几点起床？= What time do you get up?
几点上班？= What time do you go to work?

2. Number + 点 (Time)

Tell the time.
七点 = 7 o'clock
七点半 = 7:30
七点一刻 = 7:15

3. Verb + 了 (Completed Action)

Indicate completed actions.
吃饭了。= I've eaten.
下班了。= I'm off work.
