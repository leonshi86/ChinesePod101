💬 Dialogue - Lost

你:
不好意思，我迷路了。
Bùhǎoyìsi, wǒ mílù le.
Excuse me, I'm lost.
路人:
你要去哪里？
Nǐ yào qù nǎlǐ?
Where do you want to go?
你:
我要去火车站，但是找不到。
Wǒ yào qù huǒchēzhàn, dànshì zhǎo bú dào.
I want to go to the train station, but I can't find it.
路人:
不远，我带你去吧。
Bù yuǎn, wǒ dài nǐ qù ba.
It's not far, let me take you there.
你:
太谢谢了！
Tài xièxie le!
Thank you so much!

💬 Dialogue - Language Problem

你:
对不起，我听不懂。
Duìbuqǐ, wǒ tīng bù dǒng.
Sorry, I don't understand.
A:
没关系，我再说一次。
Méi guānxi, wǒ zài shuō yí cì.
No problem, I'll say it again.
你:
可以说慢一点吗？
Kěyǐ shuō màn yìdiǎn ma?
Can you speak more slowly?
A:
好的。
Hǎo de.
OK.

📝 Grammar Points

1. 可以...吗？(Can you...?)

Make polite requests.
可以帮我吗？= Can you help me?
可以说慢一点吗？= Can you speak more slowly?

2. Verb + 不到 (Can't...)

Express inability to do something.
找不到 = can't find
听不到 = can't hear
看不到 = can't see

3. 再 + Verb (Again)

Repeat an action.
再说一次 = say it again
再来一次 = do it again
