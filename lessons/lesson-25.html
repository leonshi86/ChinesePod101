Lesson 25: 社交媒体 Intermediate

Learn Chinese vocabulary and phrases for social media.

💬 Dialogue

A:
你的微信朋友圈更新了吗？我看到你发的照片了。
Nǐ de Wēixìn péngyǒuquān gēngxīn le ma? Wǒ kàndào nǐ fā de zhàopiàn le.
Did you update your WeChat Moments? I saw the photos you posted.
B:
是的，周末去了旅游。你有抖音吗？我会在上面发视频。
Shì de, zhōumò qù le lǚyóu. Nǐ yǒu Dǒuyīn ma? Wǒ huì zài shàngmiàn fā shìpín.
Yes, I went traveling this weekend. Do you have Douyin? I'll post videos there.
A:
有的。我关注你了，记得回关哦！
Yǒu de. Wǒ guānzhù nǐ le, jìde huíguān o!
Yes, I followed you. Remember to follow back!
