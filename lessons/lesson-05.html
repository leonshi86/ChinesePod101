💬 Dialogue

A:
现在几点了？
Xiànzài jǐ diǎn le?
What time is it now?
B:
现在三点半。
Xiànzài sān diǎn bàn.
It's 3:30.
A:
今天星期几？
Jīntiān xīngqī jǐ?
What day is today?
B:
今天星期一。
Jīntiān xīngqī yī.
Today is Monday.
A:
你明天有空吗？
Nǐ míngtiān yǒukòng ma?
Are you free tomorrow?
B:
有，我们几点见？
Yǒu, wǒmen jǐ diǎn jiàn?
Yes, what time shall we meet?

📝 Grammar

几点 (jǐ diǎn) - What time

现在几点？ → What time is it?
八点。 → 8 o'clock.
三点半。 → 3:30 (half past 3)

星期几 (xīngqī jǐ) - Which day

今天星期几？ → What day is today?
星期几 can mean "Monday" to "Friday" generally, but also specific days when followed by numbers.
