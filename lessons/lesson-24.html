Lesson 24: 电子邮件 Intermediate

Learn how to write professional emails in Chinese.

💬 Email Example

主题:
关于下周会议的时间安排
Guānyú xià zhōu huìyì de shíjiān ānpái
Regarding next week's meeting schedule
正文:
您好，王经理。我想确认一下下周三的会议时间。请问您方便参加吗？期待您的回复。祝好，李华
Nín hǎo, Wáng jīnglǐ. Wǒ xiǎng quèrèn yīxià xià zhōu sān de huìyì shíjiān. Qǐngwèn nín fāngbiàn cānjiā ma? Qīdài nín de huífù. Zhù hǎo, Lǐ Huá
Hello, Manager Wang. I'd like to confirm next Wednesday's meeting time. Are you available? Looking forward to your reply. Best regards, Li Hua
