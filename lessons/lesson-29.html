Lesson 29: 节日庆典 Intermediate

Learn Chinese for festivals and celebrations.

💬 Dialogue

A:
春节快到了，你回家过年吗？
Chūnjié kuài dào le, nǐ huí jiā guònián ma?
Spring Festival is coming soon. Will you go home for New Year?
B:
是的，我要回家和家人团聚，一起吃年夜饭。
Shì de, wǒ yào huí jiā hé jiārén tuánjù, yīqǐ chī niányèfàn.
Yes, I'll go home to reunite with my family and have New Year's Eve dinner together.
