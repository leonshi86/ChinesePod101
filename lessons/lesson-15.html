💬 Dialogue - Check In

前台:
您好，请问有预订吗？
Nín hǎo, qǐngwèn yǒu yùdìng ma?
Hello, do you have a reservation?
你:
有，我预订了一个房间。
Yǒu, wǒ yùdìng le yí gè fángjiān.
Yes, I booked a room.
前台:
请出示您的身份证。
Qǐng chūshì nín de shēnfènzhèng.
Please show your ID card.
你:
给你。
Gěi nǐ.
Here you are.
前台:
好的，这是您的房卡。房间在五楼。
Hǎo de, zhè shì nín de fángkǎ. Fángjiān zài wǔ lóu.
OK, here's your room card. The room is on the 5th floor.
你:
请问有无线网吗？
Qǐngwèn yǒu wúxiàn wǎng ma?
Is there WiFi?
前台:
有的，密码在这个卡片上。
Yǒu de, mìmǎ zài zhège kǎpiàn shang.
Yes, the password is on this card.

💬 Dialogue - Check Out

你:
你好，我要退房。
Nǐ hǎo, wǒ yào tuìfáng.
Hello, I'd like to check out.
前台:
好的，请把房卡给我。
Hǎo de, qǐng bǎ fángkǎ gěi wǒ.
OK, please give me the room card.
前台:
这是您的押金，请收好。
Zhè shì nín de yājīn, qǐng shōuhǎo.
Here's your deposit, please take it.
你:
谢谢，再见。
Xièxie, zàijiàn.
Thanks, goodbye.

📝 Grammar Points

1. 我要 + Verb (I want to...)

Express what you want to do.
我要退房。= I want to check out.
我要预订一个房间。= I want to book a room.

2. 有...吗？(Is there...?)

Ask about availability.
有无线网吗？= Is there WiFi?
有空房间吗？= Are there any available rooms?

3. 在 + Location

Describe location.
房间在五楼。= The room is on the 5th floor.
前台在一楼。= The front desk is on the 1st floor.

💡 Hotel Tips in China

• Passport or ID card is required for check-in
• Most hotels require deposit (押金)
• "标准间" = standard room, "大床房" = king bed room
