Lesson 17: 旅行计划 Intermediate

Learn how to plan trips, discuss travel arrangements, and talk about destinations in Chinese.

⏱ 8 minutes 📚 Intermediate 🎯 Travel Planning
0:00 / 8:00

💬 Dialogue

小李:
你暑假有什么计划吗？
Nǐ shǔjià yǒu shénme jìhuà ma?
Do you have any plans for summer vacation?
小王:
我打算去云南旅行，看看大理和丽江。
Wǒ dǎsuàn qù Yúnnán lǚxíng, kànkan Dàlǐ hé Lìjiāng.
I plan to travel to Yunnan, visit Dali and Lijiang.
小李:
听起来很棒！你打算怎么去？飞机还是火车？
Tīng qǐlái hěn bàng! Nǐ dǎsuàn zěnme qù? Fēijī háishì huǒchē?
Sounds great! How do you plan to go? By plane or train?
小王:
我打算坐高铁去，虽然时间长一点，但是可以看风景。
Wǒ dǎsuàn zuò gāotiě qù, suīrán shíjiān cháng yīdiǎn, dànshì kěyǐ kàn fēngjǐng.
I plan to take the high-speed train. Although it takes longer, I can enjoy the scenery.
小李:
你预订酒店了吗？旺季的时候酒店很紧张。
Nǐ yùdìng jiǔdiàn le ma? Wàngjì de shíhou jiǔdiàn hěn jǐnzhāng.
Have you booked a hotel? Hotels are hard to get during peak season.
小王:
已经预订了。我还在网上订了机票和景点门票。
Yǐjīng yùdìng le. Wǒ hái zài wǎngshàng dìngle jīpiào hé jǐngdiǎn ménpiào.
Already booked. I also booked flights and attraction tickets online.
小李:
行程安排得真周到！祝你旅途愉快！
Xíngchéng ānpái de zhēn zhōudào! Zhù nǐ lǚtú yúkuài!
You planned the itinerary so well! Have a pleasant trip!

📝 Grammar Points

1. 打算 (dǎsuàn) + Verb - To plan to do something

Used to express intention or plan. More casual than 计划.

Examples:
我打算去旅行。 → I plan to go traveling.
你打算什么时候出发？ → When do you plan to depart?

2. 虽然...但是... (suīrán...dànshì...) - Although...but...

A common pattern for expressing contrast. Note that unlike English, both parts are used in Chinese.

Examples:
虽然很贵，但是质量很好。 → Although it's expensive, the quality is good.
虽然下雨，但是我们还是要去。 → Although it's raining, we still want to go.

3. 已經...了 (yǐjīng...le) - Already

Indicates an action has been completed.

Examples:
我已经预订了酒店。 → I have already booked the hotel.
他已经到了机场。 → He has already arrived at the airport.

4. 行程安排 - Itinerary arrangement

Common phrase when discussing travel plans.

Examples:
你的行程安排好了吗？ → Is your itinerary arranged?
请帮我安排一下行程。 → Please help me arrange the itinerary.
