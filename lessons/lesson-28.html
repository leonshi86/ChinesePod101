Lesson 28: 音乐与电影 Intermediate

Learn Chinese for discussing music, movies, and entertainment.

💬 Dialogue

A:
你最近看什么电影了？
Nǐ zuìjìn kàn shénme diànyǐng le?
What movies have you watched recently?
B:
我刚看了《你好，李焕英》，真的太好哭了！
Wǒ gāng kàn le 'Nǐ hǎo, Lǐ Huànyīng', zhēn de tài hǎo kū le!
I just watched 'Hi, Mom', it was really so touching!
