💬 Dialogue

A:
你家有几口人？
Nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rén?
How many people are in your family?
B:
我家有五口人。爸爸、妈妈、哥哥、妹妹和我。
Wǒ jiā yǒu wǔ kǒu rén. Bàba, māma, gēge, mèimei hé wǒ.
There are 5 people. Dad, Mom, older brother, younger sister and me.
A:
你爸爸做什么工作？
Nǐ bàba zuò shénme gōngzuò?
What does your dad do?
B:
他是医生。
Tā shì yīshēng.
He is a doctor.

📝 Grammar

家有 + number + 口人

我家有三口人。= My family has 3 people.
Note: 口 is the measure word for family members.

这是我的 + family member

这是我爸爸。= This is my dad.
NOT: 这是我的爸爸 (also OK, but 这是我爸爸 is more natural)

💡 Cultural Note:
Family is very important in Chinese culture. When introducing family, start from the oldest. Father's side family uses different titles from mother's side: 爷爷/奶奶 (paternal) vs 外公/外婆 (maternal).
