Lesson 23: 商务会议 Intermediate

Learn Chinese for business meetings, presentations, and professional discussions.

💬 Dialogue

主持人:
各位好，今天的会议主要讨论下季度的销售计划。
Gèwèi hǎo, jīntiān de huìyì zhǔyào tǎolùn xià jìdù de xiāoshòu jìhuà.
Hello everyone, today's meeting mainly discusses next quarter's sales plan.
经理:
我们先来看一下上季度的数据。销售额增长了15%。
Wǒmen xiān lái kàn yīxià shàng jìdù de shùjù. Xiāoshòu'é zēngzhǎng le 15%.
Let's first look at last quarter's data. Sales increased by 15%.
员工:
请问有什么具体的目标吗？
Qǐngwèn yǒu shénme jùtǐ de mùbiāo ma?
Are there any specific goals?
经理:
我们的目标是下季度增长20%。大家有什么建议？
Wǒmen de mùbiāo shì xià jìdù zēngzhǎng 20%. Dàjiā yǒu shénme jiànyì?
Our goal is 20% growth next quarter. Any suggestions?
