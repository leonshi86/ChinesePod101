Lesson 18: 在机场 Intermediate

Learn essential Chinese phrases and vocabulary for navigating airports, checking in, and handling flight issues.

⏱ 8 minutes 📚 Intermediate 🎯 Travel
0:00 / 8:00

💬 Dialogue

乘客:
您好，我想办理登机手续。
Nín hǎo, wǒ xiǎng bànlǐ dēngjī shǒuxù.
Hello, I'd like to check in.
工作人员:
请出示您的护照和机票。有行李需要托运吗？
Qǐng chūshì nín de hùzhào hé jīpiào. Yǒu xíngli xūyào tuōyùn ma?
Please show your passport and ticket. Do you have any luggage to check?
乘客:
有一个行李箱。请问可以带多少公斤？
Yǒu yī gè xínglixiāng. Qǐngwèn kěyǐ dài duōshao gōngjīn?
I have one suitcase. How many kilograms can I bring?
工作人员:
经济舱可以托运23公斤。您的行李超重了3公斤，需要补交200元。
Jīngjìcāng kěyǐ tuōyùn 23 gōngjīn. Nín de xíngli chāozhòng le 3 gōngjīn, xūyào bǔjiāo 200 yuán.
Economy class allows 23kg. Your luggage is 3kg overweight, you need to pay 200 yuan extra.
乘客:
好的，这是我的登机牌吗？登机口在哪里？
Hǎo de, zhè shì wǒ de dēngjīpái ma? Dēngjīkǒu zài nǎlǐ?
Okay, is this my boarding pass? Where is the boarding gate?
工作人员:
是的。您的登机口是A12，请在起飞前40分钟到达登机口。
Shì de. Nín de dēngjīkǒu shì A12, qǐng zài qǐfēi qián 40 fēnzhōng dàodá dēngjīkǒu.
Yes. Your gate is A12, please arrive at the gate 40 minutes before departure.

📝 Grammar Points

1. 办理 (bànlǐ) - To handle; to process

Commonly used in formal contexts for administrative procedures.

Examples:
办理登机手续 → to check in
办理签证 → to apply for a visa

2. 需要...吗？ (xūyào...ma?) - Do you need...?

Used to ask about necessities or requirements.

Examples:
有行李需要托运吗？ → Do you have luggage to check?
需要帮忙吗？ → Do you need help?

3. 在...前 (zài...qián) - Before...

Indicates a time deadline or sequence.

Examples:
在起飞前40分钟 → 40 minutes before takeoff
在出发前 → before departure
