Lesson 30: 教育与学校 Intermediate

Learn Chinese for education and school topics.

💬 Dialogue

A:
你的孩子上几年级了？
Nǐ de háizi shàng jǐ niánjí le?
What grade is your child in?
B:
上小学三年级。他每天放学后都要做作业到很晚。
Shàng xiǎoxué sān niánjí. Tā měi tiān fàngxué hòu dōu yào zuò zuòyè dào hěn wǎn.
Third grade in elementary school. He has to do homework until late every day after school.
