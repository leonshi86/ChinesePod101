💬 Dialogue - Talking About Hobbies

A:
你的爱好是什么？
Nǐ de àihào shì shénme?
What are your hobbies?
B:
我喜欢看书和听音乐。
Wǒ xǐhuan kànshū hé tīng yīnyuè.
I like reading and listening to music.
A:
你喜欢运动吗？
Nǐ xǐhuan yùndòng ma?
Do you like sports?
B:
不太喜欢。你呢？
Bú tài xǐhuan. Nǐ ne?
Not really. How about you?
A:
我很喜欢打篮球。
Wǒ hěn xǐhuan dǎ lánqiú.
I really like playing basketball.

📝 Grammar Points

1. 喜欢 + Verb/Noun

Express what you like.
我喜欢看书。= I like reading.
我喜欢咖啡。= I like coffee.

2. 最喜欢 (Like...the most)

Express your favorite.
我最喜欢看电影。= I like watching movies the most.
你最喜欢什么？= What do you like the most?

3. 和 (and)

Connect two items.
我喜欢看书和听音乐。= I like reading and listening to music.
我有猫和狗。= I have a cat and a dog.
