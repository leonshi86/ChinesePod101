Lesson 20: 租房 Intermediate

Learn how to rent an apartment, discuss lease terms, and communicate with landlords in Chinese.

⏱ 9 minutes 📚 Intermediate 🎯 Housing
0:00 / 9:00

💬 Dialogue

租客:
您好，我看到网上有房子出租的信息，请问还能看房吗？
Nín hǎo, wǒ kàndào wǎngshàng yǒu fángzi chūzū de xìnxī, qǐngwèn hái néng kànfáng ma?
Hello, I saw your rental listing online. Can I still view the apartment?
房东:
可以的。您什么时候方便？房子是两室一厅，家具家电都有。
Kěyǐ de. Nín shénme shíhou fāngbiàn? Fángzi shì liǎng shì yī tīng, jiājù jiādiàn dōu yǒu.
Sure. When is convenient for you? It's a two-bedroom apartment with furniture and appliances included.
租客:
请问房租多少钱一个月？押金是多少？
Qǐngwèn fángzū duōshao qián yī gè yuè? Yājīn shì duōshao?
How much is the rent per month? What about the deposit?
房东:
月租3500元，押一付三。水电网费自理。
Yuèzū 3500 yuán, yā yī fù sān. Shuǐdiànwǎng fèi zìlǐ.
Monthly rent is 3500 yuan, one month deposit and three months rent in advance. Utilities are not included.
租客:
可以签一年的合同吗？如果提前退租怎么办？
Kěyǐ qiān yī nián de hétong ma? Rúguǒ tíqián tuìzū zěnme bàn?
Can I sign a one-year lease? What if I need to terminate early?
房东:
可以签一年。提前退租需要提前一个月通知，押金可以退还。
Kěyǐ qiān yī nián. Tíqián tuìzū xūyào tíqián yī gè yuè tōngzhī, yājīn kěyǐ tuìhuán.
Yes, one year is fine. Early termination requires one month notice, and the deposit can be refunded.

📝 Grammar Points

1. 房子的结构表达 - Describing apartment layout

Chinese apartments are described by the number of rooms: X室X厅 (X bedrooms, X living rooms).

Examples:
一室一厅 → one bedroom, one living room
三室两厅 → three bedrooms, two living rooms

2. 自理 (zìlǐ) - To handle oneself; at one's own expense

Indicates that something is not included and must be managed by oneself.

Examples:
水电网费自理 → Utilities are at your own expense
交通费自理 → Transportation costs are at your own expense

3. 如果...怎么办？ (rúguǒ...zěnme bàn?) - What if...?

Used to ask about hypothetical situations and solutions.

Examples:
如果提前退租怎么办？ → What if I need to terminate early?
如果迟到了怎么办？ → What if I'm late?
