💬 Dialogue - Talking About Weather

A:
今天天气怎么样？
Jīntiān tiānqì zěnmeyàng?
How's the weather today?
B:
今天晴天，很暖和。
Jīntiān qíngtiān, hěn nuǎnhuo.
It's sunny today, very warm.
A:
多少度？
Duōshao dù?
What's the temperature?
B:
二十五度左右。
Èrshíwǔ dù zuǒyòu.
Around 25 degrees.

🌧️ Checking Tomorrow's Weather

A:
明天会下雨吗？
Míngtiān huì xiàyǔ ma?
Will it rain tomorrow?
B:
天气预报说会下雨。
Tiānqì yùbào shuō huì xiàyǔ.
The weather forecast says it will rain.
A:
那要带伞。
Nà yào dài sǎn.
Then I need to bring an umbrella.

📝 Grammar Points

1. 天气怎么样？(How's the weather?)

Standard way to ask about weather.
今天天气怎么样？= How's the weather today?
明天天气怎么样？= How's the weather tomorrow?

2. 会 + Verb (Will...)

Used to talk about future events.
明天会下雨。= It will rain tomorrow.
后天会下雪。= It will snow the day after tomorrow.

3. 要 + Verb (Need to...)

Expresses necessity.
要带伞。= Need to bring an umbrella.
要穿外套。= Need to wear a jacket.
