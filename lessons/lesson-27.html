Lesson 27: 健身与运动 Intermediate

Learn Chinese for fitness, sports, and gym activities.

💬 Dialogue

A:
你经常去健身房吗？
Nǐ jīngcháng qù jiànshēnfáng ma?
Do you often go to the gym?
B:
是的，我每周去三次。我主要做有氧运动和力量训练。
Shì de, wǒ měi zhōu qù sān cì. Wǒ zhǔyào zuò yǒuyǎn yùndòng hé lìliàng xùnliàn.
Yes, I go three times a week. I mainly do cardio and strength training.
