💬 Dialogue - Asking for Directions

你:
请问，厕所在哪里？
Qǐngwèn, cèsuǒ zài nǎlǐ?
Excuse me, where is the bathroom?
路人:
往前走，然后向右拐。
Wǎng qián zǒu, ránhòu xiàng yòu guǎi.
Go forward, then turn right.
你:
远吗？
Yuǎn ma?
Is it far?
路人:
不远，就在前面。
Bù yuǎn, jiù zài qiánmiàn.
Not far, it's right ahead.

🗺️ Finding a Subway Station

你:
请问，地铁站在哪里？
Qǐngwèn, dìtiězhàn zài nǎlǐ?
Excuse me, where is the subway station?
路人:
一直往前走，过马路，地铁站就在左边。
Yìzhí wǎng qián zǒu, guò mǎlù, dìtiězhàn jiù zài zuǒbiān.
Go straight, cross the road, the subway station is on the left.
你:
谢谢！
Xièxie!
Thank you!

📝 Grammar Points

1. 在哪里？(Where is...?)

Ask about location.
厕所在哪里？= Where is the bathroom?
车站在哪里？= Where is the station?

2. 怎么走？(How to get there?)

Ask for directions.
地铁站怎么走？= How do I get to the subway station?
机场怎么走？= How do I get to the airport?

3. 往/向 + Direction + 走/拐

Give directions.
往前走 = go forward
向左拐 = turn left
往右拐 = turn right

📍 Direction Chain:

一直走 → 向左拐 → 往前走 → 到了

Go straight → Turn left → Go forward → You're there
