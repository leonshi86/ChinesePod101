💡 Pinyin Tips

Key Points:
  • zh, ch, sh, r - Curl your tongue back (retroflex)
  • j, q, x - Keep tongue tip behind lower teeth
  • ü - Say "ee" while rounding lips like "oo"
  • Third tone - Goes down then up (dipping tone)
  • Neutral tone - Light and short, no emphasis
